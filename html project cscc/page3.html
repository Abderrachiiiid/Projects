Fes and Marrakech, Morocco:

Morocco has a rich tapestry of history and culture, and Fes and Marrakech are two remarkable cities. In Fes, the intricate streets of the Fes el-Bali and the vibrant souks create an unforgettable experience. Marrakech, with its Jemaa el-Fnaa square and Majorelle Garden, offers a captivating blend of old and new. Did you explore the historic medina and its stunning palaces and souks?
